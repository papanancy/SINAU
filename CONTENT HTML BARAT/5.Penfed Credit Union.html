PenFed Overview

PenFed Credit Union offers personal loans for up to $50,000, and funding can be available within one to two business days with direct deposit. Anyone can apply for a PenFed loan, but you’ll have to join the credit union before accepting an offer. Members enjoy low interest rates and discounts on several financial products and services.
