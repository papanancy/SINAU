U.S. Bank Overview

U.S. Bank offers same-day personal loans between $1,000 and $50,000 at competitive rates. They are ideal for bank switchers because they reserve the best loan terms for existing customers. You’ll need a checking account or another product with U.S. Bank to qualify, so if you’re shopping for a new bank as well, this could be a good option.

Current U.S. Bank customers who meet other qualifications could see their funds within hours. If you’re not a customer, you can still apply for a U.S. Bank loan, but you’re limited to $25,000 — and it can take up to four business days to get your money.
