Upgrade Overview

Upgrade offers personal loans for up to $50,000 that can be deposited into your account as early as the next business day. In addition to an autopay discount, Upgrade offers savings for borrowers who open a Rewards Checking Plus account and have a portion of their debt paid directly. Applicants can choose a repayment term of up to seven years.
