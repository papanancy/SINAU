A personal loan is money borrowed from a bank, credit union or other financial institution that is typically repaid in fixed payments over a set number of months. Though many loans can take days to hit your bank account, fast personal loans can deposit your money as early as the same day.

To get a fast personal loan, you typically need only to fill out an application online or over the phone. But it’s generally best to shop around before applying. The best personal loans will usually offer lower interest rates and flexible repayment terms based on your creditworthiness. Knowing what you’re eligible for upfront can protect you from instant cash offers that might seem attractive, but come with predatory loan terms that can make it difficult to repay the loan.
