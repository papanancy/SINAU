Wells Fargo Overview

Wells Fargo offers personal loans for eligible existing customers at relatively low interest rates. Approved applicants can borrow between $3,000 and $100,000 and take up to seven years to pay them off, depending on the loan amount. Credit decisions can be made on the same day, with funding often available in one to three business days.
