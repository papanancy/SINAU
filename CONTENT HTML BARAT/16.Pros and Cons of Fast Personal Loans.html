Pros and Cons of Fast Personal Loans
If you’re in the market for a quick loan, consider these fast personal loan pros and cons before applying and committing.

Pros of Fast Personal Loans
Receive quick funding — in many cases, as soon as the same day
Typically offer lower interest rates than other fast loan options, like credit cards and payday loans
Most do not require collateral
Few restrictions on how the money can be used
Cons of Personal Loans
Harder to qualify for the best interest rates if credit is subpar
Fast funding can make it easier to borrow unnecessarily
May come with fees
Could come with a shorter repayment period, resulting in a high monthly payment
